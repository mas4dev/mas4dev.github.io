About us

Al Waseet Company is an Iraqi delivery company registered in the Baghdad Chamber of Commerce and established by Iraqi youth hands.Specialized in the field of transporting goods and express mail between all Iraqi governorates, Al-Waseet Company grew rapidly during the first six years of its career to become a brand specialized in local shipping of products and commercial goods to help companies and emerging entrepreneurs transfer their goods and products to their customers.Al Waseet has an integrated network and a wide fleet for fast and secure transportation and distribution services.
